About BankX

Bank of X shareholders earn reflections in the hottest tokens of the day…before they blow up!

|

Every time $BANKX is traded, shareholders automatically earn reward tokens as reflections.

What sets us apart: While other reflections tokens permanently tie their fates to a particular project, the Bank of X smart contract has the ability to substitute our reward token for any verified Erc-20 token contract. This unique capability allows our protocol to adjust to fluctuating market conditions and always remains relevant, in turn providing maximum value to our shareholders.

|

We’re a group crypto nerds, community leaders, coders, and outright degens who are extremely active in various defi and web3 communities. With Bank of X, we’ve set out to create a one-of-a-kind project that not only benefits holders through $BANKX and rewards tokens, but also encourages the sharing of alpha, research, and cross-token collaboration.

|

We firmly believe that any project’s greatest asset is its community, which is why we have decided to empower all $BANKX shareholders by implementing a community-driven selection process for our rewards tokens. In this same spirit, we are tapping into the immense power of strong communities by aligning ourselves with some of the most enthusiastic and productive groups in this space.
